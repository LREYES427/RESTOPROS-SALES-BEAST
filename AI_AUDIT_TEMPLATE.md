# AI Audit Cheat Sheet — The Template

*Use this as your playbook for every audit call.*

---

## BEFORE THE CALL

### Ask client to fill this out beforehand:

```
1. What does a typical day look like for you?
2. What takes up most of your time each week?
3. What do you wish you had more time for?
4. Do you currently use any of these?
   □ ChatGPT / AI chatbots
   □ Automated scheduling (Calendly, etc)
   □ Text message automation
   □ Email marketing tools
   □ CRM / customer database
   □ Social media scheduling tools
5. What's your biggest frustration with running your business?
```

---

## DURING THE CALL — Discovery Questions

### Opener (2 min)
> "Thanks for taking the time to chat. Before we dive in, tell me — what's the one thing you wish you had more of each week? More time, more money, more help?"

### Pain Point Probing (5 min)
> "You mentioned X takes up a lot of your time. Walk me through what that looks like — how do you handle [customer follow-ups / invoicing / proposals] now?"

**Key questions by category:**

**Scheduling & Communication**
- How do customers book with you?
- Do you text customers? How long does it take to respond?
- Do you have a voicemail system?

**Marketing & Content**
- How do you get new customers?
- Do you post on social media? How often?
- Do you write emails or newsletters?

**Admin & paperwork**
- How do you create proposals/quotes?
- How do you handle invoices?
- Do you track jobs in anything?

### The Close (3 min)
> "So here's what I'm hearing — [repeat back 3 main pain points]. Here's what I think could help immediately:
1. [Tool/recommendation] for [problem]
2. [Tool/recommendation] for [problem]
3. [Tool/recommendation] for [problem]

Would it be worth it to you to get this set up and working? I offer two packages..."

## Objection Handling Cheat Sheet

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I don't have time for this" | "That's exactly why I'm here. These tools are designed to SAVE you time — I'll set it up so it runs in the background." |
| "I'm not tech-savvy" | "You don't need to be. I'll walk you through it once, and it's set. Most of my clients aren't tech people — they just want tools that work." |
| "I already tried AI and it didn't work" | "That's fair — a lot of AI tools aren't set up for small business workflows. What didn't work? I can probably suggest something different." |
| "How much time will this actually save?" | "Most of my clients see 2-5 hours/week saved once things are automated. The first week or two takes some getting used to." |
| "Can my guys use this too?" | "Absolutely — that's the best part. You set it up once, and everyone benefits." |
| "What's the cost?" | "Most tools I'll recommend are free or under $20/month. The whole point is to save you more than that." |
