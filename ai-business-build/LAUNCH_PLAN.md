# Maverick's AI Consulting Playbook

---

## 1. DM Pitch to Plumbers

**Copy-paste this and personalize:**

> Hey [NAME]! 👋
>
> Quick question — are you using AI at all in your business? (ChatGPT, automated texts, scheduling bots, etc?)
>
> If not, I've been building a side biz helping small trades businesses like yours figure out how AI can save you time and maybe money. Think: automating customer follow-ups, writing job proposals faster, organizing invoices.
>
> No pitch, no pressure — I'd love to do a free 20-min call to show you what's possible. No obligation, just sharing what I've learned.
>
> Let me know if you're curious — always happy to chat shop.

**Why it works:**
- Low pressure ("quick question")
- Shows you know their world
- Free = easy yes
- "Side biz" = you're not a threat to their current vendors
