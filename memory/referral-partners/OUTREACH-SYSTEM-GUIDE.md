# 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Lead with value** — What's in it for THEM?
2. **Be specific** — "We pay $500-1000" is better than "we pay well"
3. **Local matters** — "We're right here in Tampa" beats generic outreach
4. **Show social proof** — "We just helped [COMPANY] with..."
5. **Persistence wins** — 95% of people don't respond to first contact
6. **Coffee meetings** — Get them OFFLINE ASAP

# 📞 SAMPLE TALKING POINTS

**When they ask "why should I refer you?"**
> "Three reasons: (1) We show up in 90 minutes — most restoration companies take 4-6 hours. (2) We treat your customer like our own — we don't try to steal them. (3) We pay $500-1000 per referral, same day."

**When they say "I already have a restoration company"**
> "Totally get it. But here's the thing — when your customer has water damage, they're stressed. They want someone fast. If your current guy isn't answering at 8pm on a Sunday, give us a call. We're your backup."

**When they ask "how do I refer?"**
> "Easy — just text or call me when you have a job. We'll take care of everything. And I'll drop a referral check in your mail within 48 hours."
