## 📅 Follow-Up Sequences

### Sequence 1: Initial Contact (Day 0)
- Send email
- Connect on LinkedIn
- If phone: leave voicemail

### Sequence 2: Check-In (Day 3)
- Text: "Hey [NAME], just following up on my message. No pressure!"

### Sequence 3: Value Add (Day 7)
- Share relevant content (e.g., "Here's a blog post about preventing water damage" — bonus if YOU write it!)
- Or: "Saw your [recent post]. Great insights!"

### Sequence 4: Coffee Invite (Day 14)
- "Hey, I really do think this could be mutually beneficial. Can I buy you 15 mins of coffee?"

### Sequence 5: Last Call (Day 21)
- "Totally understand if now's not the right time. Here's my card — store my number for when you need us!"

---

## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Personalize** — Mention something specific about THEIR business
2. **Lead with value** — What's in it for THEM?
3. **Be local** — "We're right here in Tampa" beats generic outreach
4. **Show social proof** — Mention other partners (with permission)
5. **Follow up persistently** — 5% respond to first contact, 80% to 5th
